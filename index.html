Inside RoomOS

Beautifully designed,
powerfully built

Interact with the real app on the right. Tap, scroll, and explore every feature.

Dashboard

Your Day, At a Glance

The home screen is your mission control. A personalized greeting, today's class schedule pulled live from your timetable, and a real-time spending widget showing your monthly outflow — all on one screen.

  • Live class schedule with room & time details
  • Monthly spending vs. budget progress bar
  • Quick-access buttons for expenses & maid share
Finance

Shared Expense Tracking

Never argue about money again. RoomOS tracks every rupee across your household with real-time bill splitting, a running "who owes whom" ledger, and monthly analytics.

  • Auto-Debits for rent, food & recurring bills
  • Spending vs. income ratio & net balance
  • Group vs. personal expense breakdowns
Household

Maid & Chore Management

Domestic help management, digitized. Log attendance with a single tap, track cycles, and let RoomOS calculate salary automatically based on actual days worked.

  • Morning & evening attendance tracking
  • "Anyone not home today?" deduction logic
  • Automatic salary calculation per cycle
Deep Analytics

Understand Your Spending

Stop guessing where the money goes. RoomOS analyzes your entire household expenditure, breaks it down by category, and creates beautiful visual charts so you can optimize your shared budget instantly.

  • Stunning category-wise spending charts
  • Complete month-over-month trend analysis
  • Individual contribution breakdowns
The Crew

Manage Your People

Keep the household running perfectly in sync. Easily invite new roommates via secure links, assign roles like Admin or Crew, and maintain a seamless digital roster of everyone who makes the house a home.

  • One-tap secure invite generation
  • Detailed profiles with custom avatars
  • Transparent hierarchy & role management
Settings & Personalization

Make RoomOS Yours

Total control over your household and your personal experience. Manage your crew with role-based permissions, personalize your profile with avatar uploads, and fine-tune exactly how the app looks and behaves.

  • Stunning Dark Mode & Light Mode themes
  • Invite links & granular Admin/Crew permissions
  • Customizable bottom navigation dock
